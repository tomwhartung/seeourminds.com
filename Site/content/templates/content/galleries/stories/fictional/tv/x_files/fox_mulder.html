In contrast to Mulder, Special Agent Dana Scully is logical and down-to-earth, and much more the type of person we would imagine the typical law enforcement officer to be.

It's easy to see that Scully and Mulder are opposites, and how the contrast between their personalities gives the show a great deal of its appeal.
