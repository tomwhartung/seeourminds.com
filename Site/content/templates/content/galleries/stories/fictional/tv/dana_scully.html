I always found it easier to relate to Special Agent Fox Mulder than to most other law enforcement characters, both fictional and in real life. However, I was still suprised to learn that he's an INFP - like me!

It can be difficult to imagine how someone like him would want to pursue a career in law enforcement, but it's easy to see how Mulder would do something like this when we learn about his sister Samantha. His feelings for her dominate his life and explain why he made his uncharacteristic career choice.

Mulder and Scully's Story

There is an excellent story on ArtsyVisions.com about Special Agents Scully and Mulder entitled Opposites Make a Powerful Team. It explains how Special Agents Fox Mulder and Dana Scully form a capable and resilient team — not despite but rather because  they have vastly different personalities.
