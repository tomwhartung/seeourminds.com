The main characters of Star Trek: The Original Series — Captain Kirk, Mr. Spock, and Dr. McCoy — were among the first fictional personalities I drew with the first version of the Groja © program.

Now that The Original Series is available on DVD, I decided to buy and rewatch it, and re-create the profiles and images.

  • It's nice to see the new image above does not look significantly different from the original image of Kirk's personality
  • It's also nice to see people are still talking about the series fifty years after it aired originally in 1966-1968

The story about Captain Kirk's spiritual portrait is now on ArtsyVisions.com. The story includes a review — which is of course quite positive — of the Remastered Original Series on DVD.

The extras on the DVD set, along with the plethora of information about the series now available on the web, make this a great time to revisit Star Trek: The Original Series!
